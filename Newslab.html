Can we make sports news more interesting so that people who are not interested in sports can be interested?

Sports News is a field where we can create a variety of news content than any other section. However, in Korea, the format of sports news is templated, (Articles that simply list the results of the game, news that shows only the winner, highlights videos focused on the score etc.), so people who are not interested in sports think sports news is boring. So we went through a variety of experiments to make sports news easy and fun.

Competitive Research

We looked into the top interactive sports news articles including FivethirtyEight, The New York Times, Washington Post etc.

Key Findings

Common strengths of good interactive news included good visualization of contents through gif animation, video, interview, and graph

User Research

We were curious about the difficulty that they face when attempting to read sports news and what they do to understand contents. So, We conducted casual, semi-structured interviews with 5 participants to understand the interest of sports news in people's lives. Some sample questions include

  • What type of sports news do you like?
  • Why look for sports news?
  • What is the most inconvenient thing to watch sports news?
  • What sports news do you think is good?

    We have developed three strategies for everyone to enjoy sports news.

    1. Sports Star Storytelling

    We interviewed Kim Yeon-kyung in Turkey with the help of our partner Spotv. We have increased the credibility of our content with the voice of the actual party.

    2. Interactive

    The content format is interactive. A variety of content, including text, images, visuals, music and digital elements, combine to create a story. We expressed Kim's technique in illustration divided by motion in seconds. And Spike 3D experience to realize Kim's true spike speed.

    3. Mobile Message Format

    We used the mobile message format to connect different content. We have structured the content so that readers can easily understand sports news by talking to Spoduck like a friend.
