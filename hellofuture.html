Hello Future is an interactive E-book about the future of designers.

Will machines take our job?, Could you say that a job as a designer is safe?

As technology continues to evolve, tools like Canva.com and Wix.com are emerging that can replace the role of web designers. These services allow you to get a high quality design without the help of designer. So how do designers accept the development of technology?

We started making this book with the worry that 'How can We survive as a designer?'. So we looked to see if there was a case of technological advance that affected the job of the designer. In the fast technological development of digital, we have arranged how designers should adapt. Readers of this E-book will be able to think about the question 'What will happen in the coming future?' and prepare for the future ahead of us.
