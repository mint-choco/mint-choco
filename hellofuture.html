Hello Future is an interactive E-book about the future of designers.

Will machines take our job?, Could you say that a job as a designer is safe?

As technology continues to evolve, tools like Canva.com and Wix.com are emerging that can replace the role of web designers. These services allow you to get a high quality design without the help of designer. So how do designers accept the development of technology?

We started making this book with the worry that 'How can We survive as a designer?'. So we looked to see if there was a case of technological advance that affected the job of the designer. In the fast technological development of digital, we have arranged how designers should adapt. Readers of this E-book will be able to think about the question 'What will happen in the coming future?' and prepare for the future ahead of us.

Objective

Utilizing the advantages of an ebook that cannot be implemented on paper, it creates an interactive ebook that helps readers reading comprehension skill improve.

  • Show links for easy access to more information and related websites.
  • Help readers navigate through the texts with ease.
  • Contain audio, video and animations, which can enhance the message.
  • Leverage gesture interaction to deliver context-sensitive information

    • Research

    Through books, blogs, and papers, we conducted research on the development of technology and changes in the design profession. Based on the data gathered, we analyzed by doing a card sorting activity and interviewed professor as well as other designers. This helped us to understand the readers better so that we could design a better contents for them.

    So, the index with six chapters was generated. Designer's Tool, Future Technology, Today's Design, Future Designer. Ultimately, with multiple iterations, we came up with a interactive E-book called Hello Future.
